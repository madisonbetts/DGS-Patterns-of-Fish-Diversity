Notes on column headings of microsatellite data
***********************************************
Fish_ID: Unique identifier for an individual brook trout
Cluster_ID: Unique identifier for a genetic cluster used in DAPC and BAYESASS
Reach_ID: Unique identifier for a 50m stream reach
The remaining columns show microsatellite markers
