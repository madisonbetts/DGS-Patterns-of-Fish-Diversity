This is the SNP dataset for the article: Population genetics reveals bidirectional fish movement across the Continental Divide via an interbasin water transfer.

This dataset includes all individuals (229) and all SNPs (125). The dataset contains 127 columns--one for population (Population), individual name (Sample.Name), and 125 SNP genotypes (columns 3-127). Each SNP genotype column contains both allele calls separated by a semicolon. Missing genotypes are designated by NA. 

We genotyped 229 cutthroat trout at a panel of 125 single nucleotide polymorphisms developed by Houston et al. 2012. For additional details on sampling, genotyping, and how data was subsequently filtered for analysis, see corresponding methods in the associated manuscript published in Conservation Genetics.
