# column information for data file: "WB 2001-2009 dryad.txt' Unique.ID: each fish was given a unique identifier Cohort: year of birth FS.Family.ID: identity of full-sib family assignment from Colony v. 1.2 Drainage: West = West Brook, western Massachusetts, USA River: WEST.BROOK = WB; WB.JIMMY = Open Large (OL); WB.MITCHELL = Open Small (OS); WB.OBEAR = Isolated Large(IL) Sample.Number: each seasonal sample was given a unique ID Section: each drainage was divided into 20 m sections numbered successively from downstream to upstream Fish.Number: fish were numbered successively within sections Species: BKT = brook trout Vial Number: ID that combines sample-section-fish numbers Sex: Male, Female, Unknown Length: fork length in mm Locus names, each locus given two columns '.1' and '.2', total of 12 loci
